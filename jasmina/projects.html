campaign for the basketball federation of slovenia June 2018
Client Sidera Agency, Huawei
Date June 2018
About The aim was to create and design an EMUI Theme for Huawei. The final product should draw parallels from and resemble the country of Slovenia.
A total number of themes I executed and made responsive were eight. The client launched them with a promotion campaign titled “Slovene talents”.
Our group designed a soft and illustrative theme with the city of Ljubljana’s skyline in the background along with over 80 customised icons.
The number of downloads in the first quarter was over 200 000. Most of them from Europe and Russia.
